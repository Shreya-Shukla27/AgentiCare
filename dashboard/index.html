About This Project

SBI Hackathon @ GFF 2026 — Focus Area: Digital Engagement (Pillar 03)

AgentiCare is an autonomous agentic AI engine for continuous customer engagement, built as a working demo prototype for the SBI Hackathon. Instead of relying on static, manually configured campaigns, it continuously evaluates customer behavioural signals, autonomously reasons over candidate actions, validates decisions against compliance guardrails, and simulates dispatching a personalised nudge — all without a human designing every trigger by hand.

Team: Team AgentiCare

Tech stack: Node.js, Express, vanilla JS dashboard, rule-based reasoning engine (designed as a stand-in for an LLM-based reasoning layer in production).
